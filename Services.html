Our Services

Product Security Assesment

The product security assessment is a comprehensive security audit of embedded systems, network services, applications, and firmware. Whether initiating a new product or deploying a solution, our experienced and skilled consultants will help you identify risks and vulnerabilities and apply solutions to mitigate security issues across your ecosystem.

IoT pentest

  • ●
    Our penetration and system analysis testing drive beyond fundamental analysis to assess the whole ecosystem of the IoT technology, wrapping every segment and how each impacts the security of the whole.
  • ●
    Our testing includes Communication and protocols, Embedded hardware and firmware,The IoT mobile application and Cloud APIs

Hardware Testing

  • ●
    We will examine the physical security and internal architecture of the device. This service may include a component indication, firmware extraction, identification of test points, and reconfiguring the device’s hardware to bypass authentication, intercept traffic, or inject commands that may pose a significant risk to your organization and clients.
  • ●
    Our critical areas of testing are, Exposed network services Physical access to the device’s UART, JTAG, SWD, etc. Device communication protocols The ability to extract memory and firmware Firmware update process security Storage and encryption of data

Protocol Analysis

  • ●
    We will test the communication protocols used in the device. Protocol testing includes testing the cryptographic security of encrypted transmissions, the possibility of capturing and modifying data transmissions and fuzzing the communication protocols. We will evaluate the safety of communication protocols and determine the risk to your organization and clients.
  • ●
    This includes capture and detailed analysis of network communications protocols, including Bluetooth/BLE Wifi 802.11, Zigbee, Z-Wave, Ethernet, CAN, SPI and I2C.

Firmware Analysis

  • ●
    We will extract and examine the firmware content to uncover backdoor accounts; buffer overflows, injection flaws, format strings, and other vulnerabilities. We will also assess the device’s firmware upgrade process for vulnerabilities and perform a secure boot review to ensure that public key encryption and upgrade functionality are certain.

Training
